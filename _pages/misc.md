## Myself

I'm a proud Kentuckian and grew up around Louisville, KY. I was raised as a
devout follower of the University of Kentucky Wildcats and come from a long line
of Kentuckians on my mother's side of the family.

I have a brilliant wife, Kristan Hilby-Papalia, who is also a talented engineer
and constantly has to put up with my research ramblings (and ramblings
otherwise). Whatever success I have is partially owed to her. We also have a
loving and somewhat lazy dog, Bowie, who has gotten pretty good at sleeping in
our labs.

Most of my free-time is spent either playing basketball or in the outdoors.

## Trips and Adventures

I'm fortunate to have a lot of fun outside my work (which is also a lot of
fun!). These days Kristan is my primary adventure partner. I also have a few
great friends who occasionally brighten up these journeys and excursions.

Below is an incomplete and unorganized collection of these moments.

### Big Sky, Montana

While Kristan was presenting her exciting work on morphing-wing airfoils at the
IEEE Aerospace Conference, I took the opportunity to enjoy the powder.
Thankfully, the conference schedule worked out such that Kristan joined quite a
few runs :-)

### Azores (Marine Robotics Summer School)

I was lucky to be invited as an instructor for the MIT-Portugal Marine Robotics
Summer School in 2022. The students, other instructors, and locals were all
incredible and truly made the trip wonderful.

Oh, and the school was held in the Azores. Kristan was able to join for the
latter end of the school, and we were able to have quite a few mini-adventures
outside the classes.

Highlights included:

- nighttime ascent of Mount Pico and waking up to catch the sunrise from the peak
- a hike around the caldera of Faial
- 3 days spent biking around São Miguel
- Seeing the creativity and excitement of the students

### Cross-country move (San Francisco → Boston)

Kristan and I both started grad school at MIT in the fall of 2019. We decided to
make a trip out of the move. Major points of the trip included:

- Yosemite (home to Upper Yosemite Falls, one of my favorite all-time hikes)
- Nevada
- Yellowstone
- Badlands, South Dakota
- Chicago (to see Kristan's family)
